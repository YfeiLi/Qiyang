FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To better understand the pricing of the product you're interested in, please call us promptly. We'll inquire about your product requirements and then provide you with an approximate figure.

+

How long for delivery?

We customize saw blades of different specifications for different customers, so production starts after placing an order. We have an efficient and mature production chain, ensuring that your ordered goods are produced and shipped at the fastest possible speed. Typically, for an order of 100,000 pieces, the production time is within 5 days.

+

How to package?

A box of metal blades contains 5000 pieces, while a box of non-metal blades contains 10000 pieces. The approximate dimensions of the box are 220mm * 220mm * the length of the blade.

+

How to obtain samples?

You can get samples for free. Just let us know the style you need, along with your address and contact information, and you'll receive the samples you desire.
